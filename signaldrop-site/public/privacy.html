Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SignalDrop handles information in connection with your use of the application and its integration with TikTok.

2. Information We Access

When you authorise SignalDrop via TikTok's OAuth flow, we access the following information:

  • user.info.basic — Your TikTok open ID and display name, used solely to confirm account identity during the authorisation process.
  • video.publish — Permission to upload and publish videos directly to your TikTok profile on your behalf.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

SignalDrop does not:

  • Collect, store, or share your TikTok password or login credentials
  • Access your TikTok followers, messages, or private data
  • Collect any personal information beyond what is listed above
  • Share any data with third parties for advertising or analytics

4. How We Use Your Information

The TikTok account information we access is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account for content publishing
  • Upload AI-generated videos to your TikTok profile
  • Maintain a valid OAuth session for automated publishing

5. Token Storage

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally in your application's environment configuration file (.env). These tokens are never transmitted to any third-party server and are stored only on the device running the SignalDrop pipeline.

6. Data Retention

We do not maintain a remote database. All authorisation tokens are stored locally on your device. You can revoke SignalDrop's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's app permissions settings.

7. Third-Party Services

SignalDrop uses the following third-party services during content generation:

  • Anthropic Claude / Google Gemini — for AI script generation
  • Microsoft Edge TTS — for text-to-speech voiceover synthesis
  • Pexels — for licensed stock footage
  • TikTok Content Posting API — for video publishing

Each of these services operates under their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke SignalDrop's access to your TikTok account at any time by visiting TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions and removing SignalDrop. This will immediately prevent any further publishing.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
