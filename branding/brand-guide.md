# SignalDrop — Brand Guide

## Brand Concept

SignalDrop is a faceless, autonomous content brand. No host. No face. Just the signal — whatever's worth dropping that week. The anonymity *is* the brand. It feels like a media outlet, not a person.

---

## YouTube Channel Description

> *SignalDrop is a mixed content channel covering whatever's worth tuning into — tech, culture, ideas, and everything in between. No host. No fluff. Just the signal.*
>
> *New drops when they're ready. Subscribe and stay tuned.*

---

## Visual Identity

### Colour Palette

| Role | Colour | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | Electric Teal / Signal Green | Feels digital, broadcast-y |
| Secondary | Deep Navy / Flat Black | Keeps it clean and anonymous |

### Logo Concept

A waveform or signal bar mid-drop — like a transmission hitting a surface. Simple enough to work as a profile picture at small sizes.

### Typography

Mono or geometric sans-serif. Recommended options:
- **Space Grotesk** — techy but approachable
- **IBM Plex Mono** — clean, editorial, slightly cold

---

## Brand Voice

- Confident but not arrogant
- Concise — no waffle
- Slightly mysterious — never over-explains itself
- Feels automated/editorial, not personal

---

## Taglines

| Option | Notes |
|---|---|
| *"Tuning in to everything."* | Original — still the strongest |
| *"The signal always drops."* | More attitude, slightly edgier |
| *"Receive the drop."* | Short and commanding |

---

## Channel Name

**SignalDrop**

Anonymous, brandable, works for mixed general content. Implies variety is the point — something is always being broadcast, and it's always worth catching.
